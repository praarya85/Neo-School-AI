# Neo-School: AI-Powered Academic Coordination

Final project for the Building AI course

## Summary

Neo-School is an AI-powered personal assistant designed for school administrators and teachers. It automates high-overhead tasks such as optimized exam seating arrangements, automated staff notifications, and academic documentation. By integrating long-term memory and local environmental context, Neo acts as a bridge between complex school data and simple, actionable administrative tasks.

## Background

* **The Problem:** School administration often involves repetitive, manual tasks like drafting notices for faculty and manually calculating seating charts. This leads to inefficiencies and human error.
* **Frequency:** These tasks occur daily (communication) and monthly (exams/events), consuming hours of staff time.
* **Motivation:** My goal is to simplify school operations through minimalist, functional AI that works seamlessly with human staff rather than replacing them.
* **Importance:** Efficient administration allows educators to focus more on teaching and less on paperwork.

## How is it used?

The solution is used by:
1. **School Administrators:** To generate schedules and staff messages.
2. **Teachers:** To receive automated updates and submit academic results through a unified interface.

The system is deployed in a school environment, accessible via web and mobile interfaces, ensuring real-time coordination even during busy exam seasons.

## Data sources and AI methods

* **Data Sources:** Student enrollment lists, teacher availability schedules, and room capacities. In the future, it could use historical performance data to suggest balanced study groups.
* **AI Methods:** * **Optimization Algorithms:** For generating seating arrangements that respect physical constraints.
    * **Natural Language Processing (NLP):** For drafting professional communications to staff.
    * **k-Nearest Neighbors (k-NN):** To categorize student needs or performance patterns.

## Challenges

* **Data Privacy:** Ensuring student and teacher records are handled securely and comply with local education regulations.
* **Human Touch:** AI cannot replace the nuanced judgment of a headmaster or teacher in sensitive personal situations.
* **Interoperability:** Integrating with existing, often outdated, school legacy systems.

## What next?

The next phase involves expanding "Neo" into a full-scale SaaS platform. I plan to launch a beta version in 10 premium schools by Q2 2026, incorporating native AI agent workflows that can handle long-term memory across academic terms.
